About Us

Our Journey, Our Passion, Our Food

The journey of Golli’s Fast Food Center began with our founder, Golli Sai Suhas, hailing from Karimnagar. A young man with big dreams and an unshakable passion for food.

One day, destiny introduced him to a Nepalese girl from Kandlakoya, belonging to the well-known Akhil Bhai family, the pioneers of Akhil Fast Food Center. What began as a meeting soon turned into love, and later into marriage, creating a bond of love and food that would last forever.

“Food tastes better when cooked with love.”

Inspired by his father-in-law’s legacy, Suhas decided to follow in his footsteps. With dedication and hard work, he started Golli’s Fast Food Center, a dream built with both love and inspiration.

“Dreams turn into reality when passion becomes your recipe.”

From a small idea to a grand vision, Golli’s Fast Food Center grew into a food empire, defeating all competitors in Hyderabad’s fast food world. Today, it proudly stands as Hyderabad’s No. 1 Fast Food Center.

Their love story is immortal, and their empire is proof that love, food, and passion can build legacies.

“From Karimnagar to Hyderabad – the taste that conquered hearts.”
